Credits

This project is a direct descendent of the work of two people: Dr. David Darts and his Piratebox project, and of one of the lead developers for Piratebox, Matthias Strubel. Matthias has been patient, thoughtful, and an amazing resource when I ran into roadblocks. Thanks to both of you for helping make LibraryBox possible.

Thanks to Matt Neer at the Lake Forest Library for the inspiration for the documentation on the .3.2 Piratebox release. I’d also like to thank Ross Singer for the coding help when I knew that something was possible but didn’t have the language needed to do it, and Andromeda Yelton for yet more coding help, editing, and making sure I didn’t sound like an idiot (much). Finally, thanks to Bo Baker, cinematographer extraordinaire, who helped me shoot the video, and has been a great brainstorming partner with this thing.
